Poster built with Photoshop, in the first semester of my first year at Waterloo.
See more info about this project at the My Projects page.

Photoshop Poster Creation

Having decided on a set theme that I would build my poster around, I worked within my comfort zone to apply bright vibrant colors against a harsh black for a very distinct and flavorful contrast. I used a smoke texture combined with an overlay of rainbow texturing over a cityscape background for a surrealistic final product. My final decorations consisted of choice in sketch-based and ornate font, and fine detailing to transition the harsh colors into a more delicate fade towards the very top.
